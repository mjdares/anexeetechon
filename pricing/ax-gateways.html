What are Ax Gateways?


Ax Gateways are ruggedized industrial edge devices designed for harsh environments. They provide protocol conversion, data aggregation, and edge computing capabilities to bridge legacy equipment with modern IIoT platforms.

Built with industrial-grade components, DIN rail mounting, and wide operating temperature ranges, Ax Gateways are perfect for factory floors, remote sites, and critical infrastructure.

Software Licensing Options

Runtime License

$49/month per gateway - Full Anexee runtime with unlimited tags, protocols, and edge logic. Includes all software updates and basic email support.

Development License

$199/month - Unlimited development and testing on virtual or physical gateways. Perfect for OEMs and system integrators building custom solutions.

Perpetual License

$1,499 one-time - Lifetime runtime license for a single gateway. Includes 1 year of updates and support. Maintenance renewal at $199/year.

Site License

Custom pricing - Unlimited gateways at a single physical site. Ideal for large factories or facilities with dozens of edge devices.
