Our Journey

From Vision to Industry-Leading Platform

The Beginning


Anexee was born from a simple observation: industrial automation was too complex, too fragmented, and too expensive for many organizations that needed it most.

Our founders, having spent years working with legacy SCADA systems and proprietary automation platforms, saw an opportunity to reimagine what industrial software could be. They envisioned a platform that would be powerful yet accessible, enterprise-grade yet affordable, and built on open standards rather than vendor lock-in.

Key Milestones

Foundation & Vision

Anexee was founded with a clear mission: democratize industrial automation. Our first prototype connected just three protocols but proved the concept could work at scale.

Platform Launch

After two years of development and testing with early adopters, we launched Anexee 1.0. The platform featured universal connectivity, real-time visualization, and cloud integration.

Global Expansion

Our customer base grew from local manufacturers to global enterprises. Anexee now powers operations across manufacturing, energy, water treatment, and smart buildings worldwide.

Continuous Innovation

Today, Anexee supports 50+ industrial protocols, AI-driven analytics, edge computing, and seamless cloud integration. We continue to evolve based on customer feedback and industry trends.

What Drives Us Forward


Customer Feedback: Every feature we build is informed by real-world use cases and customer needs. Your challenges become our roadmap.

Technology Evolution: From edge computing to AI/ML integration, we stay at the forefront of technology to ensure Anexee remains future-proof.

Industry Standards: We actively participate in standards bodies and open-source communities to ensure interoperability and industry advancement.
