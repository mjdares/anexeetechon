Energy Monitoring

Intelligent Energy Management for Industrial Operations

Transform Your Energy Strategy


Anexee's energy monitoring solution provides real-time visibility into energy consumption across your entire facility.

Track, analyze, and optimize energy usage with granular insights at the machine, line, and plant level. Reduce costs, improve sustainability, and meet compliance requirements with comprehensive energy analytics.

Core Capabilities

Real-Time Consumption Tracking

Monitor electricity, gas, water, and compressed air usage in real-time. Identify anomalies and peak demand periods instantly with sub-second data updates.

Energy Analytics & Reporting

Advanced analytics engine with customizable KPIs, trend analysis, and automated reporting. Compare performance across shifts, products, and facilities.

Cost Allocation & Billing

Allocate energy costs to specific departments, products, or processes. Support for demand charges, time-of-use rates, and utility bill validation.

Sustainability Metrics

Track carbon footprint, renewable energy usage, and environmental impact. Generate sustainability reports for compliance and corporate initiatives.

Advanced Features

Predictive Demand Management

AI-powered forecasting to predict energy demand and optimize load scheduling. Avoid peak demand charges and balance consumption across operations.

Equipment Performance Correlation

Link energy consumption to production output and equipment efficiency. Identify underperforming assets and optimize operational parameters.

Automated Alerts & Actions

Configure intelligent alerts for consumption thresholds, anomalies, and cost limits. Trigger automated responses to reduce waste and prevent overages.

Multi-Utility Integration

Unified platform for all utility types including electricity, natural gas, water, steam, and renewable sources. Single dashboard for complete visibility.
