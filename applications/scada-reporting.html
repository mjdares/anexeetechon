SCADA Reporting

Actionable Insights from Industrial Data

Transform Data into Decisions


Anexee's reporting and analytics platform turns vast amounts of SCADA data into meaningful business intelligence.

Create custom reports, dashboards, and visualizations that provide insights from shop floor to top floor. Make data-driven decisions with powerful analytics tools designed for industrial operations.

Reporting Capabilities

Custom Report Builder

Drag-and-drop report designer with no coding required. Create production summaries, downtime analysis, quality reports, and custom KPI dashboards in minutes.

Automated Scheduling

Schedule reports for automatic generation and distribution. Email, export, or publish to shared locations on daily, weekly, or monthly schedules.

Historical Trending

Query years of historical data with millisecond precision. Compare trends across time periods, equipment, and operational parameters with interactive charts.

Multi-Format Export

Export reports to PDF, Excel, CSV, or JSON formats. Integration with business intelligence tools like Power BI, Tableau, and custom applications.

Advanced Analytics

Statistical Analysis

Built-in statistical functions including mean, median, standard deviation, and correlation. SPC charts, capability analysis, and process performance metrics.

Predictive Analytics

Machine learning models for failure prediction, quality forecasting, and demand planning. Identify patterns invisible to traditional analysis methods.

Real-Time Dashboards

Live updating dashboards with drill-down capabilities. Display on wall-mounted screens, mobile devices, or embed in web portals for stakeholder access.

Context-Aware Insights

Correlate production data with ERP systems, weather data, and external factors. Understand root causes and optimize based on complete operational context.
